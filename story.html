First contact with a keyboard

CHAPTER_02 · 2019

Built my first website

A fan page for my favorite anime, hosted on a free subdomain. It was hideous and I loved it. Learned what <marquee> was. Learned why we don't use <marquee>.

CHAPTER_03 · 2020

The pivot to design

During the pandemic, picked up Figma and Adobe tools. Realized that the gap between a "working" interface and a "loved" one is craft — and craft is learnable.

CHAPTER_04 · 2023

Entered UiTM

Began my information management journey at Universiti Teknologi MARA. Met mentors and collaborators who taught me that information is only as powerful as the systems that carry it.

CHAPTER_05 · NOW

Building this portfolio

You are reading the latest checkpoint. I'm shipping projects, joining hackathons, and learning in public — one commit at a time.
